## Section 2 — Problem

### Section Header
```
Most people want to donate. They just assume it's complicated.
```

### Body
```
Bangalore generates over 4,500 tonnes of waste every day. A lot of it is perfectly usable — clothes the kids outgrew, furniture from the last flat, kitchen items you've been meaning to pass on for two years.

But donation drives feel like effort. Drop-off points are out of the way. NGOs have forms. Nobody has time.

So things sit. And eventually get thrown out.

Eco Smile was built to remove that friction entirely.
```

---

## Section 3 — Solution

### Section Header
```
What could be excess to you is essential to someone.
```

### Body
```
Eco Smile started with a simple observation: Bangalore has too much going to landfills and too many families without enough. The two problems are connected. So we built a service to connect them.

We collect from your door. We sort, clean, and where needed repair. Then Project Smile Trust — an NGO with verified distribution networks across Bangalore — gets those items to destitute families, homeless individuals, and communities that need them most.

That's the whole model. No middlemen. No mystery.
```

## Section 7 — FAQ

### Section Header
```
Common questions.
```

---

**Q: What items do you accept?**
```
Clothes, shoes, books, toys, kitchen utensils, small furniture, and general household items in usable condition. If something is broken beyond repair or heavily soiled, reach out anyway and we'll let you know.
```

---

**Q: Do you charge for pickup?**
```
No. Pickup is completely free. This is a purely donation-based service.
```

---

**Q: I'm not comfortable with strangers going through my belongings.**
```
Completely fair. You hand us bags, boxes, or wrapped items — you control what we see. We don't enter rooms or rummage through storage. You bring it to us at the door, or set it aside in advance. We take it from there.
```

---

**Q: How do I know my items actually reached someone?**
```
We work with Project Smile Trust, who have verified distribution networks. We're working on sharing WhatsApp updates once your batch has been distributed. As we grow, this transparency will only improve.
```

---

**Q: I have a large volume of items. Can you handle that?**
```
Yes. Whether it's a bag of clothes or a full room of furniture, reach out and we'll figure out the logistics together.
```

---

**Q: My apartment needs BBMP composting compliance. Can you help?**
```
That's exactly what our Composting as a Service is built for. We handle setup, training, and day-to-day management. WhatsApp us to get started.
```

---

**Q: How does Waste 2 Wealth work for schools?**
```
We visit monthly, collect segregated dry waste, weigh it, and pay based on volume. You decide: cash or a donation to an NGO partner.
```

---

**Q: Are you a registered organisation?**
```
Yes. Eco Smile is a registered waste management company operating in Bangalore, and part of the BBMP-mandated Bangalore Homeless Project through Project Smile Trust.
```

## Brand & Design Notes

### Colours
```
Primary green:   #2E6B3E
Mid green:       #4A9B5F
Light mint:      #EAF5EC
Earth brown:     #5C3D1E
Warm gold:       #C8972B
Warm grey:       #6B6B6B
Near-black:      #1A1A1A
White:           #FFFFFF
```

### Fonts
```
Headings:  Poppins SemiBold or DM Sans Bold  (Google Fonts — free)
Body:      Inter Regular / Inter Medium       (Google Fonts — free)
Accent:    Lora Italic                        (pull quotes only, use sparingly)
```

### Tone of Voice
```
- Warm but not sentimental
- Direct, not corporate
- Honest about being an early-stage organisation
- Bangalore-specific (BBMP, apartment culture, local NGO context)
- Short sentences. Plain words. Active voice.
```

### Photography Direction
```
- Real photos of pickups or sorted donation piles over stock images
- Hero image: negative space preferred so copy overlays cleanly
- Earthy, warm palette — avoid sterile white/blue tech aesthetics
- No pity-framing of beneficiaries — dignity over charity
```

### What NOT to write
```
- No: "innovative", "game-changing", "leveraging", "empowering", "supercharging"
- No: pity-driven language about recipients
- No: overclaiming scale EcoSmile hasn't reached yet
- No: generic sustainability copy ("building a greener future")
- No: guilt-based CTAs
```
